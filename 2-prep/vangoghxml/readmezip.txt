This zip file contains the XML documents for the letters of Vincent van Gogh as edited in:

Leo Jansen, Hans Luijten, Nienke Bakker (eds.) (2009), 
'Vincent van Gogh - The Letters'. Amsterdam & The Hague: Van Gogh Museum & Huygens ING. 
http://vangoghletters.org

It also includes the TEI ODD file and the schema generated from that file.
